PC Building Society Rules

Rule #1: No Offensive Materials

Members of the PC Building Society are expected to maintain a respectful and inclusive environment. Any form of explicit or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.

Rule #2: Respect for Others

All members are expected to treat each other with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of offensive behavior towards others will not be tolerated.

Rule #3: No Spamming

Members should refrain from spamming or flooding the community with unnecessary or irrelevant content. This includes repetitive posts, excessive self-promotion, or any form of disruptive behavior.

Rule #4: Intellectual Property Rights

Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Do not share or distribute copyrighted material without proper authorization or permission.

Rule #5: No Advertising

Members should refrain from advertising or promoting products or services without prior approval from the community administrators. This includes direct advertising, affiliate links, or any form of commercial solicitation.

Rule #6: PC Building Tutorial

All members must complete a PC building tutorial before joining the PC Building Society. This tutorial can be completed by watching the video provided to the left or any other approved tutorial. It is important for members to have a basic understanding of PC building to ensure a positive and productive experience within the community.
