Prebuilt PC's

Feeling overwhelmed by the world of custom PC builds? Don't worry, we understand that not everyone has the time or inclination to dive into component selection. That's why we've compiled a list of solid prebuilt options. Enjoy the convenience of a ready-to-go system without sacrificing too much on performance.

  • Slightly Used Toshiba Laptop


    Uhhh.... no clue to be honest

    This laptop has seen better days... and frankly, those days are long gone. It might not turn on, some of the screen is missing, and who knows what's going on with the keyboard. BUT, it's free! So if you're a tinkerer, love a challenge, or need spare parts, this could be your next PC.

  • Yeyian Yumi


    Intel Core i5 12400F | Nvidia GeForce RTX 4060 | 16GB DDR4-3200 | 1TB SSD

    This budget-friendly PC packs a serious punch! Equipped with an Intel Core i5 12400F processor and an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 graphics card, it delivers smooth performance for gaming and everyday tasks. The ample 16GB of RAM and spacious 1TB SSD ensure fast loading times and plenty of storage. All this for just £800 – a fantastic value for the price-conscious gamer.

  • ABS Stratos Aqua


    Intel Core i5 14400F | Nvidia GeForce RTX 4060 Ti | 32GB DDR5-6000 | 1TB SSD

    This powerhouse features the reliable Intel Core i5 14400F processor, future-proof NVIDIA RTX 4060 Ti graphics, a generous 32GB of DDR5-6000 RAM, and a spacious 1TB SSD. Enjoy smooth performance and stunning visuals.

  • Corsair Vengeance i7400


    Intel Core i9 13900K | Nvidia RTX 4090 | 64GB RAM | 2TB SSD

    The Corsair Vengeance i7400 is a gamer's dream machine. Dominate the latest titles with its cutting-edge Intel Core i9 13900K processor and the peerless NVIDIA RTX 4090 graphics card. 64GB of RAM ensures seamless multitasking and lightning-fast load times, while the massive 2TB SSD provides ample space for your game library and media.
